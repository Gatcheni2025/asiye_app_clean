Welcome to Asiye. We are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data.

Personal Data: We collect your name, surname, vehicle details, and phone number during signup.

Location Data: To provide our service, we collect your real-time geographic location. This is essential for matching you with passengers and tracking parcels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to operate the Asiye service, connect you with commuters, process payments, and ensure physical safety.
